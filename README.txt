It is my finall project in Maktab50 using Django and some other techs..
It is a simple CRM.
It has several applications :
    1- company : It`s about our company (like our products)
    2- organization : It`s about our customers that are several organizations.(list of organizations and their products)
    3- experts : It`s about our staff specially our sell experts.(experts Authentication and Profile and Permissions)
    4- sellProcess : It`s about our company`s sell process.(quote and follow-up)
    5- email : It is for email implementation.(sending email using celery task)
